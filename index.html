About Us

About us: Supple Enterprise Group is an international holding company with its roots in South Africa and headquarters in Cape Town. It has a growing number of investments in logistics, cleaning chemicals, mining, agriculture, transport, and health. We actively engage with all our teams to provide strategic guidance to identify high growth potential opportunities within the Group. We apply a hands-on approach across all operational activities to promote and enhance a well-diversified team.

Values: Our Stakeholders, Partners, as well as our employees, are our greatest asset in the Group. We are constantly reminding each other to walk and practise this Motto in our Companies “Better than yesterday" meaning we are constantly upgrading and developing our services and the relationships we have with our clients as well as our suppliers and partners.

Vision: To promote job creation for low grade semi-skilled industrial and commercial workers seeing the immerging rise of technological advancement. Even though we also innovate meaning we are of technology being a young vibrant company we highly endorse Human Capital.
